Core capabilities

  • Built-in agent types: Greedy, Cautious, Explorer (extendable)
  • Bandwidth & timestep knobs to model communication constraints
  • Batch runs and exportable CSV/JSON for full research workflows
  • Dual-mode: Animated demo for learning (≤10) — Data mode for research-scale runs
  • Pluggable RL agents (Q-Learning) and statistics toolkit

About

SwarmSim is designed to let researchers, engineers, and curious minds explore emergent coordination. Small demo runs are animated and approachable; large experiments focus on performance and robust analytics. Use the controls to craft scenarios or click Try App for the full interactive console.
